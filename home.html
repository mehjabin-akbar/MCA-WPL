Welcome to Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS), located in Varikoli near Kochi, Kerala, is a premier engineering college established with a vision to provide quality technical education and foster innovation among young minds. The institute is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and offers NBA-accredited programs in various engineering disciplines.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-equipped library, experienced faculty members, and strong industry connections, MITS ensures a holistic learning environment. The college emphasizes not only academic excellence but also co-curricular and extracurricular activities, creating a vibrant campus life for students.

MITS is committed to producing socially responsible engineers who can contribute effectively to technological advancements and nation building.

Vision Mission
To be a centre of excellence for learning and research in engineering and technology, producing intellectually well-equipped and socially committed citizens possessing an ethical value system.
  • Offer well-balanced programme of instruction, practical exercise and opportunities in technology.
  • Foster innovation and ideation of technological solutions on sustainable basis.
  • Nurture a value system in students and engender in them a spirit of inquiry.
