KOCHI / COCHIN

Welcome to Kochi

Kochi, also called Cochin, is one of the most beautiful and historically significant cities in India. It is located in the state of Kerala, on the southwest coast along the Arabian Sea. Known as the “Queen of the Arabian Sea,” Kochi has a unique blend of cultural heritage, modernity, and natural beauty. Over centuries, it has been influenced by Portuguese, Dutch, and British rule, making it a city rich in history and diversity.

Kochi is a thriving centre for commerce, culture, and tourism. It is famous for its scenic backwaters, historic buildings, art galleries, and vibrant festivals. The city’s cultural landscape includes Kathakali dance performances, traditional boat races, and spicy Kerala cuisine that attracts visitors from around the world. Kochi is also a gateway to Kerala’s tourist attractions, making it a favourite destination for travellers.

Famous Places in Kochi

Marine Drive

A scenic waterfront promenade in Kochi, offering beautiful views of the backwaters and the city skyline. It is popular for evening walks, street food, and boat rides.

Fort Kochi

A historic seaside area in Kochi known for its colonial architecture, quaint streets, and iconic Chinese fishing nets. It is a cultural hub with art galleries, cafés, and rich heritage, making it a must-visit destination.

Jew Town

A historic area in Kochi near Mattancherry Palace, famous for its antique shops, spice markets, and the Paradesi Synagogue. It reflects Kochi’s rich cultural heritage and attracts tourists for its unique blend of history and charm.

Mattancherry Palace

Also known as the Dutch Palace, located in Mattancherry, Kochi. It is famous for its beautiful Kerala murals, antique artifacts, and royal history, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Kerala.

Kerala Kathakali Centre

A cultural hub in Fort Kochi showcasing Kerala’s traditional dance-drama Kathakali, along with music and martial arts. It offers daily performances and workshops for visitors to experience Kerala’s rich heritage.

Bolgatty Palace

A historic Dutch-era palace located on Bolgatty Island in Kochi, now converted into a luxury hotel. Surrounded by beautiful gardens and offering views of the backwaters, it is a popular spot for history lovers and tourists alike.
