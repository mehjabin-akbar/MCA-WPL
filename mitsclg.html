About Us

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS), promoted by Muthoot M. George Institute of Technology, is a self-financing technical institution in Kochi offering undergraduate and postgraduate engineering programmes.

MITS is affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, approved by AICTE, and offers B.Tech, MCA, and MBA programmes. Backed by the 130+ year legacy of the Muthoot Group, the institute carries forward values of trust and excellence, with the group now spanning 18 divisions, 4,500+ branches, and a global presence
Vision Mission
To be a centre of excellence for learning and research in engineering and technology, producing intellectually well-equipped and socially committed citizens possessing an ethical value system.
  • Offer well-balanced programme of instruction, practical exercise and opportunities in technology.
  • Foster innovation and ideation of technological solutions on sustainable basis.
  • Nurture a value system in students and engender in them a spirit of inquiry.
